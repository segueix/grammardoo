What's a Superlative? 🤔

Use it when something is #1 among 3 or more — the best, the biggest, the most beautiful. Always with THE!

🥈
taller
🥇
the tallest
🥉
tall

tall (1 thing) → taller (vs 1) → the tallest (the winner!)

Examples 👀

🏔️Everest is the tallest mountain in the world.
🍕Pizza is the best food! (irregular)
📕This is the most interesting book.
😄Today is the happiest day of my life!

The Formula ✨

the + adjective + est
OR
the + most + adjective
STEP 2 OF 7 · LEARN

3 Rules + Monsters 🎯

1
SHORT word (1 syllable) → the + word + est
tall→the tallest
fast→the fastest
big→the biggest
nice→the nicest

💡 big → biggest (double) · nice → nicest (just +st)

2
Ends in -y → y becomes -iest
happy→the happiest
easy→the easiest
funny→the funniest
busy→the busiest
3
LONG word → the most + word
beautiful→the most beautiful
expensive→the most expensive
difficult→the most difficult
interesting→the most interesting

🐉 Irregular Monsters

Same monsters as comparatives — just learn them!

good→the best
bad→the worst
far→the farthest
little→the least
