STEP 2 OF 7 · LEARN

⏰ Prepositions of TIME

Only 3 to remember — but you need to know which one for what:

IN
months
years
seasons
parts of day
ON
days
dates
weekends
AT
clock times
night
weekend (UK)

👀 Time examples

📅My birthday is in July.
📆School starts on Monday.
🕗We meet at 8 o'clock.
☀️I run in the morning.
🌙I sleep at night. (irregular!)
❄️It snows in winter.

⚠️ Tricky cases!

Watch out for these — they look like exceptions:

🏠at home / at school / at work
🌙at night (NOT in the night)
🎉on my birthday / on Sunday morning
