What is "Present Simple"? 🤔

It's the tense we use for things that happen again and again — your routines, habits, and facts that are always true.

📅

Think: "Every day…" / "Every week…" / "Always…"

Examples 👀

🏫I go to school every day.
🌅The sun rises in the east.
🎮He plays video games on Sundays.
🍕We love pizza.
STEP 2 OF 7 · LEARN

The Magic Formula ✨

Subject + verb
(but add -S for He / She / It!)

⚠️ The famous third-person -S — never forget it!

Which form to use?

I
→ I play
You
→ You play
He / She / It
→ He plays
We / They
→ We play

💡 Only He / She / It gets the extra -S. Everyone else uses the plain verb!

📜 Spelling rules for -S

+s play → plays
+es watch → watches
o → oes go → goes
y → ies study → studies
