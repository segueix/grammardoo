What's a comparative? 🤔

It's an adjective that compares two things. To compare, we add -er or use more, plus the magic word than.

🆚

Examples 👀

🐘An elephant is bigger than a mouse.
🚗This car is faster than mine.
📚Maths is more difficult than art.
🍕Pizza is better than salad. (irregular!)

The Magic Formula ✨

Thing 1 + is + comparative + than + Thing 2

3 Rules + Monsters 🎯

1
SHORT word (1 syllable) → +er
tall→taller
fast→faster
big→bigger
nice→nicer

💡 big → bigger (double consonant) · nice → nicer (just +r)

2
Ends in -y → y becomes -ier
happy→happier
easy→easier
busy→busier
funny→funnier
3
LONG word (2+ syllables) → more + word
beautiful→more beautiful
expensive→more expensive
difficult→more difficult
interesting→more interesting

🐉 Irregular Monsters

These don't follow ANY rule — just learn them!

good→better
bad→worse
far→farther
little→less
