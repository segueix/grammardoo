What is "Past Simple"? 🤔

It's the tense for actions that already finished in the past. They started, they ended — it's over!

⏪

Time signals: yesterday, last week, in 2020, ago, when I was…

Examples 👀

🍕I ate pizza yesterday.
⚽She played football last Saturday.
🏖️We went to the beach in summer.
🎂He was born in 2012.
STEP 2 OF 7 · LEARN

Two Kinds of Verbs ⚔️

Subject + verb in past
(same form for everyone — no -S problems here!)

🟢 Regular verbs: just add -ED

+edplay → played
ends in e: +dlive → lived
y → iedstudy → studied
double + edstop → stopped

🔴 Irregular verbs: they change!

You just have to learn them. Here are the most common monsters:

go→went
eat→ate
see→saw
have→had
do→did
make→made
get→got
come→came
